Activities

Many of Club Penguin Island's activities are social, fun games designed to bring penguins together. From outdoor activities like sledding or riding on a jetpack, visitors can also practice their Card-Jitsu skills against other great ninja penguins. If you're lucky, you may even be recruited by the PSA to help complete top-secret missions.
